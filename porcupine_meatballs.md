# Porcupine Meatballs

## Ingredients

### Meatballs
| Ingredient | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Ground Beef | 2 pounds |
| Instant Rice | 1 cup |
| Milk | 1 cup |
| Onion | 1 |
| Salt | 2 teaspoons |
| Pepper | 1 teaspoon |
| Celery Salt | 1 teaspoon |
| Garlic Salt | 1 teaspoon |

### Sauce
|Ingredients | Amount |
| --- | --- |
| Tomato Sauce | 4 cups |
| Water | 2 cups |
| Worcestershire Sauce | 2 tablespoons|

## Instructions

Mix beef, rice, milk, onion, and seasonings and form into meatballs. Lightly brown in a fry pan with a little butter. Add meatballs to sauce and simmer for 45 minutes.
